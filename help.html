🙋 Help & Support – AR Libraries

Welcome to the Help Center of AR Libraries! This page is created to guide you through every step of using our platform. Whether you are a first-time visitor trying to access notes, a student looking to upload resources, or someone facing an issue, this page will answer your questions. AR Libraries is designed to be simple, user-friendly, and student-focused.

📌 1. Getting Started

AR Libraries is a digital platform where students can upload, search, and download study materials such as notes, e-books, and previous question papers.

  • Visit the Homepage to explore available resources.
  • Use the Search Bar to quickly find notes by subject or semester.
  • Go to the Upload Section if you want to contribute your study materials.

📌 2. How to Upload Notes, E-Books, and Question Papers

  • Go to the Upload Form.
  • Enter the following details: Title, Subject, Semester, File Link.
  • Click the Upload button.

✅ Once uploaded, your note will instantly appear in the resources list.

📌 3. How to Search & Find Study Materials

  • Type the subject name (e.g., “DBMS”).
  • Enter the semester (e.g., “3rd Semester”).
  • Use keywords to filter specific topics.

📌 4. Downloading Notes & Using Counters

  • Find the resource you want.
  • Click the Download Button.
  • The file will open or download depending on the browser.

📊 Each download increases the Download Counter.

📌 5. Rating Study Materials

  • Find the note you used.
  • Click the Rate Button (★).
  • Higher rating = more trusted resource.

📌 6. Common Problems & Solutions (FAQs)

  • Q1: My uploaded note is not showing.
    👉 Refresh the page.
  • Q2: I uploaded the wrong file link.
    👉 Delete and re-upload.
  • Q3: Why are downloads not counting?
    👉 Use the Download Button.
  • Q4: Can I upload large files?
    👉 Yes, use links from Google Drive/Dropbox.
  • Q5: Do I need an account?
    👉 Currently, no account is required.

📌 7. Best Practices for Students

  • Upload clear, well-structured notes.
  • Give accurate titles and subjects.
  • Share correct and working file links.
  • Rate other students’ resources.
  • Respect shared content.

📌 8. Future Features

  • User Profiles – Track uploads and downloads.
  • Discussion Forum – Clear doubts.
  • Admin Approval – Teachers check quality.
  • Mobile App – Access AR Libraries on devices.

📌 10. Conclusion

AR Libraries is a student-driven digital ecosystem providing easy access to study materials. By following best practices, you help build a stronger knowledge-sharing community. Thank you for being part of AR Libraries!
