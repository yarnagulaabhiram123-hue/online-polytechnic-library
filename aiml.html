Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) is a rapidly growing field that focuses on creating intelligent systems capable of learning, adapting, and making decisions with minimal human intervention. AI enables machines to perform tasks such as understanding natural language, recognizing patterns, and problem-solving, while Machine Learning provides the techniques for systems to learn from data and improve over time. This course equips students with knowledge of programming, data handling, neural networks, and real-world applications in areas like robotics, healthcare, finance, and automation.
